About Abdul Razak

Born in Daharki, Sindh, into the respected Bhutto family, I grew up in an environment that valued education and service. My father, Abdul Hakeem Bhutto, has always been my guiding light, fostering a household of academic pursuit alongside my brothers, Abdul Nasir and Abdul Wali Khan.

I am a Computer Scientist by trade and an MPhil Research Scholar by passion. My journey in technology began in the local classrooms of Daharki and has led me to the National College of Business Administration & Economics (NCBA&E).

Beyond the technical realm, I have a deep interest in education, exemplified by my Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) from Ammara College. I believe in the power of technology to uplift communities, a principle I apply to every software solution and research paper I produce.
